About Us

We Are Committed to Farmers' Success

FarmGenius is an AI-driven agriculture platform designed to remove middlemen and empower farmers with knowledge, fair pricing, and sustainable practices.

It provides farmers with direct market access, soil and crop intelligence, chemical safety guidance, and digital financial literacy in their own language.

The platform combines AI chatbot, soil testing integration, weather forecasting, and government scheme awareness to ensure farmers get higher yield, more income, and safer farming.
