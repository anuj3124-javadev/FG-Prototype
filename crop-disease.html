Tomato Early Blight 92% confidence

Description: Fungal disease that affects leaves, stems, and fruits of tomato plants.

Recommended Solutions:

  • Remove infected leaves and dispose of them properly
  • Apply copper-based fungicides every 7-10 days
  • Improve air circulation around plants
  • Water at the base of plants to avoid wetting foliage
Prevention Tips for Future
  • Rotate crops annually to prevent disease buildup
  • Use disease-resistant tomato varieties
  • Ensure proper spacing between plants
  • Apply mulch to prevent soil splashing onto leaves
